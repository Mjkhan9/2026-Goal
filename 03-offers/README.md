# 03 — Offers

Where opportunities become **offers**: a clear promise, a clear buyer, a clear price, a clear delivery.

## Subfolders

- `opportunities/` — raw ideas under evaluation. Graduate them to `drafts/` or kill them.
- `drafts/` — offers being shaped. Each draft should state: who it's for, outcome, scope, price, proof, delivery.
- `active/` — offers currently in market. These are the only ones being actively sold.

## Standard structure for an offer file

```
# <Offer name>

- Status: opportunity | draft | active | retired
- ICP (who):
- Outcome (what they get):
- Scope (what's included / excluded):
- Price & structure:
- Delivery timeline:
- Proof required to sell it:
- Risks / unknowns:
```

## Rules

- Only graduate to `active/` once there is evidence of buyer interest.
- Retired offers move to `99-archive/offers/` with a short post-mortem.
