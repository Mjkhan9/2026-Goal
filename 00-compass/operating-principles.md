# Operating Principles

Non-negotiable rules that govern how work is done. Update only when a
principle genuinely changes.

## Core principles

1. **Sell outcomes, not AI.** Buyers pay for business results. AI is
   the leverage, not the product.
2. **Files over chats.** Any insight, decision, or artifact worth
   keeping lives in this repo.
3. **Judgment is the scarce resource.** AI compresses labor; the
   operator owns direction, quality, and trust.
4. **Diagnose before you sell.** Offers emerge from per-business
   research. Scope is set per engagement until accumulated diagnostic
   data earns a productized offer.
5. **Prove before systemizing.** Deliver manually to real buyers
   before turning anything into a system or product.
6. **Focus by phase, not by offer.** During Phase 1 the active bet is
   accumulating diagnostics. Later phases focus on whatever the data
   has earned. Do not mistake phase focus for premature narrowing.
7. **Cheap to kill, fast to ship.** Validate with the smallest honest
   version; discard quickly if signal is weak.
8. **No fabrication.** Case studies, metrics, testimonials, and claims
   must be real.
9. **Label your claims.** Mark every recommendation as one of:
   (a) current best hypothesis, (b) reasoned assumption, (c) validated
   pattern. Do not blur them.

## How to use this file
If a decision contradicts a principle here, either the decision is
wrong or the principle needs to be updated explicitly via a decision
record in `01-state/decisions/`.
