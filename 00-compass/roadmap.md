# Roadmap

Phased arc for the initiative. Each phase has a goal and a rough exit
condition. Exit conditions are provisional — they exist to prevent premature
advancement, not to create false precision.

## Current phase: Phase 1 — Research & Diagnose

---

## Phase 1 — Research & Diagnose

**Goal:** Build diagnostic volume on Houston-area SMBs. Understand what
problems actually exist in the market before committing to a specific offer.

**Work:**
- Source candidate businesses (publicly visible, Houston-area, SMB).
- Complete per-business diagnostic briefs using `02-research/houston-smb-diagnostics/_template.md`.
- Log patterns in `_pattern-log.md` as they emerge.
- Identify which intervention types recur and which seem most actionable.

**Rough exit condition:** ~20 diagnostic briefs completed and at least one
clear pattern has emerged that justifies narrowing. This number is a guideline,
not a hard gate — the real signal is pattern clarity, not count.

---

## Phase 2 — Deliver

**Goal:** Take the intervention type(s) surfaced by Phase 1 diagnostics and
deliver real engagements to paying buyers.

**Work:**
- Draft an offer around the validated intervention type(s).
- Source and contact buyers who match the ICP the diagnostics revealed.
- Deliver engagements manually. Document outcomes.
- Write a delivery SOP only after the second or third engagement confirms
  the workflow is stable.

**Rough exit condition:** 3+ paid engagements delivered, documented, and
outcomes are real. At least one delivered the same way twice.

---

## Phase 3 — Narrow & Systemize

**Goal:** Convert the most repeatable engagement type into a defined,
SOP-driven offer with stable scope and pricing.

**Work:**
- Tighten scope and pricing based on delivery experience.
- Build reusable delivery assets in `05-delivery/` and `06-assets/`.
- Explore whether a specific vertical or buyer type improves efficiency.

**Rough exit condition:** Offer delivered consistently 3+ times, delivery time
meaningfully shorter than engagement 1, pricing stable.

---

## Phase 4 — Recurring / Productize

**Goal:** Turn proven delivery into recurring revenue or a productized
offering — only after Phase 3 signal is real.

**Work:** Defined by what Phase 3 reveals. Could be retainers, a managed
service, a template pack, a tool, or something else. Not worth speculating
until Phase 3 is complete.

**Entry condition:** Phase 3 exit met.
