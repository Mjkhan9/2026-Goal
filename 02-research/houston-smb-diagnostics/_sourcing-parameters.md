# Sourcing Parameters

Defines how to find and filter candidate businesses for diagnostic briefs.
Complete and lock this file before building `_candidate-pool.md`.

---

## Geography
- **Target:** Houston, TX metropolitan area
- **Includes:** Houston proper + inner suburbs (Sugar Land, Katy, Pearland,
  Pasadena, The Woodlands, Humble, Friendswood, League City, Baytown, etc.)
- **Excludes:** _(fill: any areas to avoid, e.g. national chains, outside
  metro)_

## Business size
- **Target:** Small businesses — roughly 1–50 employees
- **Signals to use (if headcount not visible):** solo/owner-operated storefront,
  single-location service business, minimal corporate web presence, owner
  is the face of the brand
- **Excludes:** franchises, national chains, businesses with dedicated
  marketing teams (typically visible from site quality / LinkedIn)

## Sector filters
- **Open to any sector in Phase 1** — the goal is pattern discovery, not
  vertical confirmation
- **Suggested starting sectors** (high density, locally owned, likely
  underpresenced online):
  - Home services (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, cleaning, landscaping)
  - Health & wellness (chiropractors, massage, physical therapy, med spas)
  - Food & beverage (independent restaurants, food trucks, catering)
  - Professional services (accountants, attorneys, consultants, real estate)
  - Auto services (repair, detailing, tinting)
  - Fitness (gyms, personal trainers, studios)
  - Beauty (salons, barbershops, nail)
- **Excludes:** _(fill: any sectors to skip — e.g. highly regulated,
  already heavily digitized, etc.)_

## Publicly visible requirement
- Every business in the pool must be identifiable from public sources.
- Acceptable sources: Google Maps, Yelp, Facebook Business, LinkedIn,
  Chamber of Commerce directories, industry directories.
- Do not fabricate or guess business details.

## Minimum signals required to add to pool
- [ ] Business name confirmed
- [ ] Physical or service location confirmed as Houston-area
- [ ] At least one public digital touchpoint visible (website or GBP)
- [ ] Appears to be independently owned (not a franchise or chain)
