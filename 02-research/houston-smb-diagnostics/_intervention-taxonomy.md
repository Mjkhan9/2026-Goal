# Intervention Taxonomy

Reference list of intervention types used when logging gaps in diagnostic
briefs. Keep this broad. Do not retire or narrow categories until diagnostic
volume justifies it — a category that rarely appears is still useful as a
"confirmed absent" signal.

---

## Categories

### Website
Fixes to an existing site or building a basic presence from scratch.
Examples: landing page rebuild, mobile responsiveness, speed, clear CTA,
contact friction reduction.

### Booking / Scheduling
Implementing or improving online booking, appointment flows, or scheduling
automation.
Examples: Calendly integration, booking widget on site, automated reminders.

### Chatbot / Conversational
Adding a lightweight conversational layer to handle FAQs, capture leads,
or route inquiries.
Examples: site chatbot, SMS auto-responder, WhatsApp business setup.

### Lead Capture
Improving how a business collects and follows up with inbound interest.
Examples: lead form, email opt-in, offer/incentive, CRM connection.

### Reviews / Local SEO
Improving Google Business Profile presence, review volume, and local
search visibility.
Examples: GBP claim/optimize, review request automation, citation cleanup.

### Automation
Reducing manual work through simple workflow automation.
Examples: inquiry → follow-up email, booking → reminder, form → spreadsheet.

### Content / Media
Improving how a business presents itself through basic content assets.
Examples: photo refresh, service descriptions, before/after documentation.

### Analytics
Adding basic visibility into what is and isn't working.
Examples: GA4 setup, GBP insights review, call tracking.

### Integrations
Connecting tools that are currently siloed.
Examples: booking system → calendar, form → email list, POS → reporting.

---

## Notes
- One business can have gaps in multiple categories.
- Rank by likely ROI in the diagnostic brief, not by category order here.
- Log pattern frequency in `_pattern-log.md`, not here.
