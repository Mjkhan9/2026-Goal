each brief. Used to decide when to narrow — and what to narrow to.

**Narrowing rule:** Do not select a specific offer or vertical based on
fewer than ~10 diagnostic briefs. Patterns that appear in 1–2 businesses
may be noise. Patterns that appear in 5+ businesses across different sectors
are signal worth acting on.
